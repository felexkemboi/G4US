About Us

Feel,Think and Fix

After undergraduate course,according to Kenyan tradition its time to look for a job.But the story outside there in the job market,the story is not as expected.The knowledge acquired back in school is totally different from what is expected in the job market.This made me tarmac for years not getting an employer who is willing to take me.I realized that the problem is experienced by most fresh undergraduates.This compelled me to come up with this idea of bridging the gap.Starting with my former university,Pwani university,we will be working to have an incubation centre in each university to provide practical skills.Also we will be having an incubation centre where we will be solving problems affecting the environment and coming up with innovations that help to solve problems within the environment
